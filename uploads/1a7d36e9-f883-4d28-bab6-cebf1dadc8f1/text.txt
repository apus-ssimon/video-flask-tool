What’s shaping the future of fitness and wellness?
From national health initiatives to global trends, this course dives into the forces transforming how we move, live, and thrive.
You’ll explore how professionals in health science impact the populations they serve—and what credentials and boundaries define their roles.
We’ll connect the dots between Healthy People 2030 goals and the real-world challenges facing the U.S. population.
Discover how to evaluate fitness trends, assess client needs, and design programs that work—for everyone.
You’ll learn to separate science from hype—and build wellness strategies that are ethical, effective, and evidence-based.
By the end, you’ll be equipped to think critically, act professionally, and lead with knowledge in a fast-evolving industry.
